The Brains & Brawn Musicians
  • The Brains & Brawn consists of husband and wife duo Joseph and Shannon Souza. Building soundscapes of melodic rock and hints of chillwave

DJ Matty Matt DJ / Producer
  • From the early days of his DJ career starting in 2007 in Australia to the college bars and college parties he rocked from 2009-2011 in Northfield
  • MN. Since 2011 Matty has established his place as a professional turntablist DJ in the Minneapolis night club scene playing every major nightclub in Minneapolis.
  • Currently Matty is holding two residencies at two of the hottest nightlife spots in downtown as well as being a DJ for the Minnesota Timberwolves Wild Twins and Vikings with Generation Now entertainment. He knows how to let the music and the art of being a DJ shine through in every one of his sets.

Mister Black DJ Producer
  • MIster Black began his musical journey at the youthful age of 8
  • with a drum kit and a need for musical expression. This need formed the basis for what would become the foundation of his music career. Not one to rest
  • Mister Black explored many personal interests before finding what would become his next noise-making instrument. With a strong sense of rhythm

  • Mister Black continues to maintain ongoing residencies and appearances at premier local and national venues.
